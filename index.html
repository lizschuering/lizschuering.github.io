About Me

Hello world, my name is Liz! By day I help run operations for a Chicago-based dev shop and by night I'm a student in Northwestern University's part-time coding bootcamp.

I'm fascinated by how the web and technology products have reshaped our daily lives in such fundamental ways and I've always wanted to look under the hood and see how it all works. I'm excited to see where my journey into coding takes me over the course of the next six months and to share some of the things that I've been building along the way.
